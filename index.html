About me

Marla Mockus is currently a student at Denver University in the Web Development Full-Stack bootcamp! She enjoys yoga, paddle boarding, and travel. Marla went to Santa Barbara City College, where she studied Studio Art with an emphasis in clay sculpture. She also attended FIDM and studied textile design. Marla loves the childlike excitment gained from paying attention to the smaller details around her. As a certified yoga teacher, and sound healer connection to mind, body, and spirit is important to her. Marla is always striving to learn and grow, and is excited for this next chapter in life as she steps into becoming a Web Developer.
